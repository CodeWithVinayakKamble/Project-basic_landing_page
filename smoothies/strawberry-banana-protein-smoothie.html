Strawberry Banana Protein Smoothie

-: Description :-

This strawberry-banana protein smoothie with almonds and protein powder is great for a meal replacement or after a workout. It is packed with natural fruit flavors, healthy fats from almonds, and a rich chocolate protein boost to keep you fueled and satisfied!

Ingredients :-

  • 1 Â¼ cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 10 whole almonds
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate flavored protein powder

Steps :-

  1. Place strawberries, banana, almonds, and water into a blender; blend to combine. Add ice cubes and puree until smooth. Add protein powder and mix until evenly incorporated, about 30 seconds.
